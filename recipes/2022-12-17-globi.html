Globi

This is very simple recipe. It doesn't originally feature salt, but adding some won't hurt.

Ingredients

  • Normal wheat flour
  • Ricotta. It really needs to be ricotta. Or else you won't be making globi.
  • Honey
  • Poppy seeds
  • Olive oil, if you have the skills to control the heat.
  • Normal cooking oil, if you're a filthy barbarian.

Steps

  1. Mix the ricotta and the flour on a bowl until you get an ok dough.
  2. Make balls with it, 3 to 4 cm of diameter.
  3. Deep fry them.
  4. Once they're done cover them with honey and poppy seeds.
