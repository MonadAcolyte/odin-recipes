Thick Broth Carbonara Ramen

This is how you can turn a normal ramen pack into the glorious TBC-ramen.

I reccomend that you use samyang's carbonara pack, as that will provide the better result.

Ingredients

  • Ramen pack
  • An egg
  • Leek
  • Chillies (if your ramen pack isn't spicy)
  • Sausages (Vegan or Carcass)
  • Tofu
  • Ghee

Steps

  1. Fill a pot with as much water as a bowl can hold and set it to boil.
  2. Add the broth sackets within the ramen pack, if you're cooking samyang add the powder and the sauce together.
  3. Cut the tofu in a slice that can cover 60% of the bowls top area and throw in the broth.
  4. On a pan, fry the leek with some Ghee. Then set it aside.
  5. On the pan, fry the sausages. Preferably, cut them into octopus shape before frying them. Once thy're done, set them aside.
  6. Once the tofu has boiled in the broth for a good 5mins, fry is as well and set it aside.
  7. Lastly boil the noodles in the broth. During the 2mins that they take to cook, scramble the egg.
  8. Turn off the fire and throw the egg on the noodles, stir vigorously utill the egg fully merges with the broth.
  9. Now it's time to put everything together. The tofu goes on top, everything else goes on the broth.
